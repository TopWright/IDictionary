Definition

organisation

//ɔːɡ(ə)nʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n/

NOUN
  1. an organized group of people with a particular purpose, such as a business or government department. "a research organization"
  2. the action of organizing something. "the organization of conferences"

Use Cases

  • We can't do without the help of your organisation.
  • His application for membership of the organisation was rejected
  • The organisation has been enjoined to end all restrictions.
  • The prospect of change of this kind has an unsettling effect on any organisation.
